Capanes Del Golf - 2 Bedroom Apartment

Rent: €1800 Purchase: €520,000

Nestled in the exclusive Capanes del Golf, this contemporary 2-bedroom apartment is an idyllic haven within a gated community, commanding breathtaking views of the Marbella Club golf course. The residence boasts exceptional amenities, featuring five outdoor pools, a wellness area with an indoor pool, spa, and gym.

Positioned on the second floor, the spacious unit with en-suite bathrooms in both bedrooms offers a serene ambiance on the large terrace, perfect for unwinding or al fresco dining. With private parking spaces in the garage and outside, convenience is paramount.

Located in Benahavis, a short drive leads to the charming village's tapas bars, renowned golf courses like El Higueral, and beautiful beaches in San Pedro, Puerto Banus, and Estepona. Additionally, the proximity to Puerto Banus and Marbella ensures access to upscale shopping at boutiques and the El Corte Ingles department store.

The development provides 24-hour security, making it an ideal lock-and-leave prospect for a tranquil and secure lifestyle.
