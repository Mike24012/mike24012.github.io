Nestled in the prestigious "Urbanización La Alquería" in Capanes Sur, Benahavís, this minimalist villa offers an extraordinary living experience. Surrounded by multiple golf courses and only a five-minute drive from the beach, the villa is ideally positioned to capture stunning views of both the sea and the cliffs. The area's mild climate, paired with exceptional Mediterranean gastronomy, makes this villa the perfect retreat for those seeking a peaceful and luxurious lifestyle.

This contemporary villa is a masterpiece of timeless architecture, designed to harmonize with its natural surroundings. The villa features clean, forceful lines and is perfectly oriented to maximize sea views from every room. Expansive glass joinery enhances the sense of space and light, creating a seamless connection between the interior and the lush, native flora-filled gardens. The swimming pool, elegantly integrated into the garden, further blurs the lines between indoor and outdoor living, offering a serene environment for relaxation.

The villa's location in La Alquería is not just prestigious but also a wise investment. As part of one of Costa del Sol’s most sought-after gated communities, this property is poised to appreciate in value over time. The villa’s proximity to world-class amenities, including golf courses, beaches, and the vibrant cultural scene of Puerto Banús, ensures a lifestyle of convenience and exclusivity. Whether you're enjoying a quiet evening at home or entertaining guests, the villa’s sophisticated design and breathtaking views make every moment extraordinary.

Spanning four levels, the villa is designed for ultimate comfort and luxury. The basement offers sheltered parking for two vehicles and can be fully customized to suit your needs. On the ground floor, light-filled spaces open onto a beautifully organized garden with a pool and spacious porch. The first floor features bedrooms, each with its own private bathroom, while the panoramic terrace on the roof provides stunning views of the entire Costa del Sol, making it the perfect spot for relaxation and enjoyment.
